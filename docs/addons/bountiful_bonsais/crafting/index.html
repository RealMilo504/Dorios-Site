Crafting and Setup

Before your bonsai can start producing resources, you’ll first need to craft a Bonsai Pot and understand how it works.

Crafting the Bonsai Pot​

The Bonsai Pot is the core block of the addon.
It acts as the growing platform where all bonsais are planted.

Recipe:

  • 3 × Stone Slabs
  • 1 × Hopper
  • 2 × Planks

Once crafted, it’s ready to be placed anywhere.

Placing and Using the Bonsai​

Place your bonsai on top of a container, such as a Chest or Barrel.
This allows it to automatically store all generated items — no hopper is required.

Each bonsai collects its drops directly in the block beneath it.
You can use any container that supports item storage, including UtilityCraft blocks.

Soil Setup​

Every bonsai requires a soil to grow.
Different soils affect growth speed and compatibility with plants.

Growth Speed Modifiers​

Soil TypeEffect
Dirt / GrassNormal growth
Red Sand / Sand10s faster
Hoed SoilAdditional 10s faster

Using a hoe on soil increases growth speed — except on special soils from UtilityCraft or Bountiful Crops, which already have unique properties.

Compatible Soils and Plants​

Soil TypeCompatible Plants
Dirt / GrassVanilla trees, crops, berries, mushrooms
Sand / Red SandCactus, Sugar Cane, Kelp
Crimson NyliumCrimson Tree
Warped NyliumWarped Tree
EndstoneChorus Fruit
Soul SandNether Wart

The base growth time for any bonsai is 60 seconds, reduced further by certain soils and upgrades.

Planting a Tree​

Once you’ve added soil, take a sapling or seed and interact with the bonsai to plant it.
If placed correctly, a miniature version of the plant will appear.

Your bonsai will now begin to grow and automatically produce resources.

💡 Tip: You don’t need a hopper below the bonsai.
It will automatically transfer items to the container beneath it.

Removing or Resetting​

You can remove:

  • Only the bonsai using shears (keeps the soil)
  • Both bonsai and soil by sneaking and breaking it with your hand
