Dorios' Trinkets

The Dorios' Trinkets addon introduces a collection of over 80 unique trinkets — rings, amulets, charms, pendants, and more — each designed to grant special abilities and stat boosts that enhance your adventures across the world of Minecraft.

With a dedicated trinket inventory system, players can equip and manage their accessories seamlessly. From extra health and speed to magic protection and life steal, every trinket brings new strategic depth to gameplay.

Trinket System Overview​

Trinkets can be equipped through a custom inventory menu or directly from your hand.
To access the inventory, interact while holding the Trinket Scroll, craftable using paper.

Available Slots​

The addon includes 13 unique slots for maximum customization:

Slot TypeExamples
HeadHelmets, Masks
NecklacePendants, Amulets
BodyEmblems, Quivers
FeetBoots
RingsNormal and Heavy Rings
CharmsAbyssal Orbs, Purity Blossoms
TalismansHoly Cross, Bloodgem
GauntletsFire, Ice, Venom variants
DollsVoodoo-type trinkets
AmuletsBloodbound, Abyssal Sun, etc.

Trinket Tiers​

TierDescription
PrimitiveBasic trinkets with simple effects, ideal for early-game.
ApprenticeIntermediate trinkets with stronger abilities.
MastercraftAdvanced trinkets offering powerful effects.
AscendantTop-tier trinkets with game-changing bonuses.
Ascendant+Beyond Ascendant – the rarest and strongest trinkets.

Responsive stat bonuses stack dynamically.
Example: +10 health and -2 health trinkets result in a total of +8 health.

Abilities & Effects​

Trinkets provide a variety of benefits and powers, such as:

  • Increased health, damage, and speed
  • Enhanced water mobility and magic protection
  • Life steal during combat
  • Immunity to negative status effects
  • Item repair and double jump abilities

Compatibility​

Dorios' Trinkets is fully compatible with:

  • Archaism Talismans
  • Archaism Trinkets
  • Amulets Vanilla

They share the same core system, allowing combined use without issues.

Technical Info​

  • Works on Minecraft Bedrock 1.21.50+
  • Does not require experimental toggles
  • Uses a player.json-based stat system for all bonuses
  • 100% compatible with multiplayer and realms
