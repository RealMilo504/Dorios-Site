Dorios Excavate - Release

October 7, 2025 · 2 min read
Milo504
Owner

Tired of chopping trees or mining ores one block at a time?
Dorios Excavate introduces a smarter, faster, and achievement-friendly way to dig through Minecraft Bedrock.

Smarter Mining​

This addon reimagines how players mine and gather resources.
Instead of breaking blocks one by one, Excavate lets you instantly mine entire veins, trees, or tunnels — all while keeping lag to a minimum.

Each block is processed with a short delay to maintain smooth performance even during large excavations, making it ideal for both survival and multiplayer servers.

Full Control, Your Way​

Every player can fully customize their excavation experience:

  • Add or remove blocks from your whitelist
  • Toggle the excavate ability on or off
  • Change your Shape Mode (Line, Tunnel, Vein, etc.)
  • Adjust your Block Limit for performance and balance

Works With Achievements​

One of Dorios Excavate’s strongest features is its compatibility with achievements.
That means you can use it in your survival worlds without disabling Xbox Live achievements — making it perfect for both casual and competitive players.

Performance-Focused​

Unlike traditional vein miners, Dorios Excavate was designed to be lag-friendly.
By spreading block-breaking operations over short intervals, it ensures stable tick performance even when excavating massive areas.

Available Commands​

excavateadd              – Adds a block to your personal excavate whitelist.
excavateblacklistadd     – Adds a block to the global blacklist. (Admin only)
excavateblacklistremove  – Removes a block from the global blacklist. (Admin only)
excavatedefaultadd       – Adds a block to the global default list. (Admin only)
excavatedefaultremove    – Removes a block from the global default list. (Admin only)
excavatelimit            – Sets your personal block limit for excavate.
excavateopenmenu         – Opens your personal excavate configuration menu.
excavateremove           – Removes a block from your personal excavate whitelist.
excavateshape            – Changes your current excavate shape or pattern.
excavatetoggle           – Toggles your excavate ability on or off.```

UtilityCraft 3.2 — The Automation Update

October 7, 2025 · 3 min read
Milo504
Owner

Release Date: October 7, 2025
Developed by: Dorios Studios
Download: UtilityCraft v3.2.0 on GitHub

Overview​

UtilityCraft 3.2 delivers one of the most extensive updates to date, focusing on deeper automation, improved stability, and technical reworks for long-term addon compatibility.
This release rebuilds core systems for machines, energy transfer, and fluid management, while introducing a new generator, refined UIs, and developer integration through ScriptEvents.
Players can now enjoy more control, faster performance, and flexible automation that scales with their world.

Major Additions & Changes​

Machines​

  • Reworked sieving system with tiered meshes and balanced drop progression.
  • Added Copper Cobblestone Generator as the first tier in a six-level structure.
  • Rebalanced energy and speed across multiple machines including Crusher, Infuser, Digitizer, and Induction Anvil.
  • New unified machine interface with progress display, upgrade slots, and information area.

Generators​

  • Added the Wind Turbine, a passive generator whose efficiency varies with height.
  • Generators now show detailed information such as time of day, remaining fuel, and efficiency.
  • Introduced transfer modes for energy, items, and fluids: Nearest, Farthest, and Round.

Tools and Items​

  • Added Steel and Copper AiOTs, Hammers, and Paxels.
  • Added Copper Mesh and new compressed item families.
  • Updated textures for tools, machines, and Mob Grinder.

Mob Grinding​

  • Mob Grinders are now upgradeable with Damage and Range modules.
  • Can be toggled on or off for improved control.
  • Enhanced entity detection and performance.

Technical Overhaul​

  • Full rewrite of machine, generator, and fluid systems for efficiency and modular design.
  • Introduced ScriptEvent integration to allow external addons to register, replace, or modify recipes dynamically:
    • utilitycraft:register_crusher_recipe
    • utilitycraft:register_infuser_recipe
    • utilitycraft:register_melter_recipe
    • utilitycraft:register_crafter_recipe
    • utilitycraft:register_fuel
    • utilitycraft:register_sieve_drop
  • Added utilitycraft:mesh component for defining custom sieve meshes.
  • Updated rate speed settings:
    • Super Fast → 2 ticks
    • Fast → 4 ticks
    • Normal → 10 ticks
    • Slow (Default) → 20 ticks
    • Super Slow → 40 ticks
  • Reduced maximum stack size of upgrades to 8.

Compatibility​

UtilityCraft 3.2 maintains full compatibility with other Dorios Studios projects and popular technical addons such as Integrated Storage and Drawers, ensuring a seamless automation network between systems.
