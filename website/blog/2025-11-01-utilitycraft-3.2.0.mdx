## Overview

UtilityCraft 3.2 delivers one of the most extensive updates to date, focusing on deeper automation, improved stability, and technical reworks for long-term addon compatibility.  
This release rebuilds core systems for machines, energy transfer, and fluid management, while introducing a new generator, refined UIs, and developer integration through ScriptEvents.  
Players can now enjoy more control, faster performance, and flexible automation that scales with their world.

---

## Major Additions & Changes

### Machines
- Reworked sieving system with tiered meshes and balanced drop progression.
- Added Copper Cobblestone Generator as the first tier in a six-level structure.
- Rebalanced energy and speed across multiple machines including Crusher, Infuser, Digitizer, and Induction Anvil.
- New unified machine interface with progress display, upgrade slots, and information area.

### Generators
- Added the Wind Turbine, a passive generator whose efficiency varies with height.
- Generators now show detailed information such as time of day, remaining fuel, and efficiency.
- Introduced transfer modes for energy, items, and fluids: Nearest, Farthest, and Round.

### Tools and Items
- Added Steel and Copper AiOTs, Hammers, and Paxels.
- Added Copper Mesh and new compressed item families.
- Updated textures for tools, machines, and Mob Grinder.

### Mob Grinding
- Mob Grinders are now upgradeable with Damage and Range modules.
- Can be toggled on or off for improved control.
- Enhanced entity detection and performance.

---

## Technical Overhaul

- Full rewrite of machine, generator, and fluid systems for efficiency and modular design.
- Introduced ScriptEvent integration to allow external addons to register, replace, or modify recipes dynamically:
  - `utilitycraft:register_crusher_recipe`
  - `utilitycraft:register_infuser_recipe`
  - `utilitycraft:register_melter_recipe`
  - `utilitycraft:register_crafter_recipe`
  - `utilitycraft:register_fuel`
  - `utilitycraft:register_sieve_drop`
- Added `utilitycraft:mesh` component for defining custom sieve meshes.
- Updated rate speed settings:
  - Super Fast → 2 ticks
  - Fast → 4 ticks
  - Normal → 10 ticks
  - Slow (Default) → 20 ticks
  - Super Slow → 40 ticks
- Reduced maximum stack size of upgrades to 8.
