  The <b>Dorios' Trinkets</b> addon introduces a collection of over 80 unique trinkets — rings, amulets, charms, pendants, and more — 
  each designed to grant special abilities and stat boosts that enhance your adventures across the world of Minecraft.
</p>

<p>

</p>

Trinkets can be equipped through a <b>custom inventory menu</b> or directly from your hand.  
To access the inventory, interact while holding the **Trinket Scroll**, craftable using paper.

### Available Slots

The addon includes **13 unique slots** for maximum customization:

| Slot Type | Examples |
|------------|-----------|
| Head | Helmets, Masks |
| Necklace | Pendants, Amulets |
| Body | Emblems, Quivers |
| Feet | Boots |
| Rings | Normal and Heavy Rings |
| Charms | Abyssal Orbs, Purity Blossoms |
| Talismans | Holy Cross, Bloodgem |
| Gauntlets | Fire, Ice, Venom variants |
| Dolls | Voodoo-type trinkets |
| Amulets | Bloodbound, Abyssal Sun, etc. |

---

## Trinket Tiers

| Tier | Description |
|------|--------------|
| **Primitive** | Basic trinkets with simple effects, ideal for early-game. |
| **Apprentice** | Intermediate trinkets with stronger abilities. |
| **Mastercraft** | Advanced trinkets offering powerful effects. |
| **Ascendant** | Top-tier trinkets with game-changing bonuses. |
| **Ascendant+** | Beyond Ascendant – the rarest and strongest trinkets. |

> Responsive stat bonuses stack dynamically.  
> Example: +10 health and -2 health trinkets result in a total of +8 health.

---

## Abilities & Effects

Trinkets provide a variety of benefits and powers, such as:

- Increased **health**, **damage**, and **speed**  
- Enhanced **water mobility** and **magic protection**  
- **Life steal** during combat  
- **Immunity** to negative status effects  
- **Item repair** and **double jump** abilities

## Compatibility

Dorios' Trinkets is **fully compatible** with:
- **Archaism Talismans**
- **Archaism Trinkets**
- **Amulets Vanilla**

They share the same core system, allowing combined use without issues.

## Technical Info

- Works on **Minecraft Bedrock 1.21.50+**  
- Does **not require experimental toggles**  
- Uses a **player.json-based stat system** for all bonuses  
- 100% compatible with **multiplayer** and **realms**
