## Feeding Water to the Thermo Generator

The Thermo Generator consumes **water** to prevent overheating and maintain stable operation.

### Manual Interaction
Use a **Water Bucket** to manually fill the generator — ideal for quick setups or testing.

### Fluid Transport Systems
You can automate water input using **Fluid Pumps** or **Fluid Extractors** connected with **Fluid Pipes**.

- **Fluid Pump:** The fastest and most efficient method.  
  Can draw water from a **Sink**, **Fluid Tank**, or even **source blocks**.  
- **Fluid Extractors:** Slower but more flexible — multiple units can supply water steadily.

The Thermo Generator interface displays its **stored energy**, **water level**, and **operational status**.  
It updates in real time depending on the available water and the heat source below.  
If either requirement is missing, the generator will pause until conditions are restored.
