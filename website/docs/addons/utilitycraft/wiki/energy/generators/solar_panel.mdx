## Energy & Storage Capacities

| Tier | Rate (DE/t) | Energy Capacity |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| **Basic** | 5 DE/t | 16,000 DE |
| **Advanced** | 20 DE/t | 64,000 DE |
| **Expert** | 80 DE/t | 256,000 DE |
| **Ultimate** | 500 DE/t | 1,600,000 DE |

---

## Behavior

The Solar Panel only produces energy **during the day** when exposed to direct sunlight.  
It will automatically stop generating if itâ€™s **nighttime**, **raining**, or **covered by blocks**.  
All stored energy is **retained when broken**, allowing it to be safely relocated without loss.

---

## Solar Panel UI

The Solar Panel interface displays its **current energy production rate** and **stored Dorios Energy (DE)**.  
It updates dynamically based on sunlight availability and the generator's tier.  
If light levels drop or the sky becomes obstructed, the production will temporarily pause.
