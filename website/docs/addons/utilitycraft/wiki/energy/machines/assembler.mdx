## How It Works

The Assembler is an automated crafting machine that uses **Blueprints** to create items without manual input.

To use it:
1. Insert a **Blueprint** with a saved recipe into the **top slot**.  
2. Fill the **crafting grid** with the exact materials required by the recipe.  
3. The machine will automatically start producing the item based on the inserted Blueprint.

---

## Speed Upgrade Behavior

Unlike other machines, the **Assembler** increases its **output per operation** instead of reducing craft time.

| Upgrade Level | Items Crafted per Operation |
|----------------|------------------------------|
| 0 (No Upgrades) | 4 |
| 1 | 8 |
| 2 | 16 |
| 3 | 32 |
| 4 | 64 |

ðŸ“Œ Each additional **Speed Upgrade** doubles the batch size, making it perfect for **mass production** setups when paired with high-tier energy and item systems.

---

### How to Use

1. Place the **Assembler** in your world.  
2. Insert a **Blueprint** from the **Digitizer**.  
3. Add all the **required materials** in the crafting grid.  
4. Ensure enough stored energy (**1,600 DE per operation**).  
5. *(Optional)* Add **Speed Upgrades** to increase crafting batch size.
