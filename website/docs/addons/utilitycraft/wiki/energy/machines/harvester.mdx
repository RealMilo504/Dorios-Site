The **Harvester** is a machine that automates crop and block harvesting in a configurable area.  
It consumes energy per block harvested, making it efficient for farms of any size â€” especially when combined with hoppers or transport systems for collection.

---

### Machine Information

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| **Energy Cap** | 32,000 DE |
| **Energy Cost** | 100 DE per block harvested |

ðŸ’¡ **Tip:** With Range Upgrades, the Harvester can cover up to a **9x9** area.  
At **Range Level 4**, items are dropped **behind the machine** for easy collection with hoppers.

## Range Levels

| Range Level | Area Covered | Drop Behavior |
|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| 0 | 3x3 | Drops on the ground |
| 1 | 5x5 | Drops on the ground |
| 2 | 7x7 | Drops on the ground |
| 3 | 9x9 | Drops on the ground |
| 4 | 9x9 | Drops behind the machine |
