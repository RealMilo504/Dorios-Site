# Autosieve

<div style={{ display: "flex", alignItems: "center", gap: "1.5rem" }}>

    src="/img/addons/utilitycraft/energy/autosieve_render.png"

  <div>
    <p>
      The <b>Autosieve</b> is a machine that automatically sifts <b>gravel, sand, dirt</b> and other sieveable materials to extract valuable drops.  
      It requires <b>Dorios Energy (DE)</b> and a <b>mesh</b> to operate efficiently.
    </p>
    <p>
      The Autosieve can be upgraded with <b>Speed Upgrades</b> to reduce processing time, and its efficiency increases depending on the amount of energy upgrades installed.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

## Machine Data

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| Energy Capacity | 512,000 DE |
| Energy Cost per Operation | 6400 DE |
| Base Processing Rate | 160 DE/t |
| Accepts Speed Upgrades | Yes |

## Autosieve UI

The Autosieve interface displays its **energy storage**, **mesh slot**, and **progress bar**.  
Once powered and provided with a mesh, it will automatically process any valid block inserted into its input slot.
