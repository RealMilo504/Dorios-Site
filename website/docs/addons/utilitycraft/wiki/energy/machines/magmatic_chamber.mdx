## Extracting Lava

To extract the produced lava, place a **tank** or **Magmator** in front of the machine.  
It will automatically transfer the lava without requiring extra input.

You can also use **Fluid Pumps** and **Exporters** to move the lava wherever itâ€™s needed.

This setup allows for a **near-infinite lava source**, ideal for feeding Magmators and generating continuous energy.
