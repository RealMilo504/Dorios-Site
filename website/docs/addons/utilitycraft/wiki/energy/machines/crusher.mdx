# Crusher

  <div>
    <p>
      The <b>Crusher</b> is a machine that processes <b>ores, blocks, ingots, and plates</b> into their dust or simplified forms.  
      It is designed for automation, ore doubling, and efficient material processing.
    </p>
    <p>
      The Crusher consumes <b>800 DE per operation</b> and can be upgraded with <b>Speed Upgrades</b> to increase its processing rate.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

## Machine Data

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| Energy Cap | 64,000 DE |
| Energy Cost per Operation | 800 DE |
| Base Processing Rate | 20 DE/t |
