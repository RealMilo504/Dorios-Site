<p>
  <b>Dorios' Basics</b> is part of the <b>UtilityCraft</b> addon pack and focuses on improving early-game survival and resource gathering.  
  It introduces tools like <b>Pebbles</b>, <b>Hammers</b>, <b>Sieves</b>, <b>Crucibles</b>, and <b>Sinks</b> — essential mechanics for 
  Skyblock-style starts or minimalist progression worlds.
</p>

This module provides the foundation for UtilityCraft’s early progression loop.  
Players can generate resources from minimal materials and automate their basic survival setups.


---

## Pebbles

Use a <b>Wooden</b> or <b>Stone Hammer</b> while sneaking and interacting with the ground to collect small fragments called <b>Pebbles</b>.

These can be recombined into full blocks, making it possible to rebuild stone resources even in Skyblock-like environments.

| Source Block | Pebble Type |
|---------------|-------------|
| Grass / Dirt | Dirt Pebble |
| Stone | Stone Pebble |
| Gravel | Gravel Pebble |
| Granite | Granite Pebble |
| Diorite | Diorite Pebble |
| Andesite | Andesite Pebble |

---

## Hammers

Hammers are the cornerstone of resource progression.  
They allow players to break blocks down into smaller materials:

| Input | Output |
|--------|---------|
| Cobblestone | Gravel |
| Gravel | Dirt |
| Dirt | Sand |
| Netherrack | Crushed Netherrack |
| Cobbled Deepslate | Crushed Deepslate |
| End Stone | Crushed End Stone |

> Combine hammers with **Sieves** for optimal ore farming.

## Flint Knife

Craft **Flint** using 3 Gravel directly in your inventory, then make a **Flint Knife**.  
Use it to harvest **Plant Fibers** from leaves.

- Combine 3 Fiber → **String**  
- Enables early crafting recipes before you can hunt spiders.

---

## Mortar — Getting Water

The **Mortar** extracts water from saplings and is essential for early hydration.

**Crafting Recipe:**
- 3 Cobblestone  
- 1 Stone Pebble  

**Usage:**  
Place **2 Saplings**, **1 Glass Bottle**, and **1 Mortar** in your inventory crafting grid to create a **Water Bottle**.  
The mortar is **not consumed**.

## Sieve

The **Sieve** filters blocks such as gravel, dirt, or sand to extract valuable materials and seeds.  
Place a mesh inside, add a siftable block on top, and start filtering!

- Works in **groups up to 5x5 (25 sieves total)**.  
- Interacting with one activates all adjacent sieves at once.

## Crucible

Use the **Crucible** to convert cobblestone into **Lava** or **Obsidian** using different heat sources.

| Heat Source | Strength |
|--------------|-----------|
| Torch | 1 |
| Fire / Campfire / Magma | 2 |
| Soul Fire / Soul Torch | 3 |
| Lava | 4 |

> Place cobblestone in the crucible, wait for it to melt, then collect with a bucket.
